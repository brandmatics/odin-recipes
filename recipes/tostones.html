Tostones

Description

Tostones are crispy fried green plantains. Serve as a side dish with your meal or as an appetizer. Tostones are delicacy from the carribean islands, the tostones, add a great twist to every dish.

In this recipe, I'll show you how to cook, prepare, and present a fresh plate of tostones

Ingredients

  • Green Plantain
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Peel plantain and cut into 1-inch slices. Fill bowl with 3 cups of cold water.
  2. Heat oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat; add platian slices in an even layer and fy on both sides until gold brown, about 3.5 minutes per side. Set skillet aside.
  3. Transfer plantain slices to a chopping board; flatten each one by placing a small plate on top and presesing down. Dip plantain slices in cold water.
  4. Reheat oil in the skillet over medium heat; cook plantain slices for 1 minute on each side. Season to taste and serve immediately.
